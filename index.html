Find Your Lost Items with Ease

PING2RETRIEVE is an online lost and found system for OMSC Mamburao Campus. Report lost items or browse found items to help reunite them with their owners.

Features

��

Easy Search

Search through found items with advanced filters and categories to quickly locate your lost belongings.

📝

Simple Reporting

Report lost items with detailed descriptions and photos to increase chances of recovery.

🔔

Instant Notifications

Get notified when your lost item is found or when a potential match is identified.

How It Works

1

Report Your Lost Item

Fill out a detailed form with information about your lost item, including description, location, and photos if available.

2

Search Found Items

Browse through the database of found items or use the search function to look for your lost item.

3

Get Notified

Receive notifications when your lost item is found or when a potential match is identified.

About PING2RETRIEVE

A centralized web-based platform for reporting and recovering lost items at Occidental Mindoro State College - Mamburao Campus.
